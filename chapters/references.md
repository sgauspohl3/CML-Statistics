## Suggested further reading

- *All of Statistics* — Larry Wasserman. Compact, modern, mathematically serious.
- *Statistical Rethinking* — Richard McElreath. A Bayesian textbook that prioritizes intuition.
- *Bayesian Modeling and Computation in Python* — Martin, Kumar, and Lao. A practical Bayesian workflow textbook.
- *The Art of Statistics* — David Spiegelhalter. Accessible, statistics-as-a-way-of-thinking.
